📖 How RBTL Works

Step 1: Analyze the last 5 days of draws (seed period)

Step 2: Find "Hot Months" - historical months where those patterns appeared

Step 3: Extract all candidates from those hot months

Step 4: Rank candidates by 3DP (3-digit pair) matches with seed draws

Step 5: Return Top 10-30 suggestions with highest scores


✅ Result: 47.7% of the time, a winner appears in Top 3 predictions!
